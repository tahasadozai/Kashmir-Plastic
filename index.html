Kashmir Plastic Works is Pakistan's trusted manufacturer of high-quality plastic cans, serving industries across the country with durable and cost-effective packaging solutions. From 1L to 20L, our products are designed for strength, safety, and efficiency.

About Kashmir Plastic Works

Established with a mission to deliver quality and reliability, Kashmir Plastic Works is a leading plastic can manufacturing company based in Pakistan. We specialize in producing a wide range of HDPE plastic cans and containers, designed to serve industrial, commercial, and household needs.

Our manufacturing facility is equipped with modern machinery and a skilled workforce, allowing us to meet high-volume demands without compromising on quality. Every product we produce is built to withstand pressure, resist chemicals, and maintain shape — ensuring safe storage and transport for various liquids and materials.

We are committed to continuous improvement, customer satisfaction, and sustainable practices. Whether you’re a small distributor or a large-scale supplier, Kashmir Plastic Works is your trusted partner in plastic packaging.

Our Top Products

These are our top and most popular products, consistently in demand across all seasons.

12-liter water can

Our 12-liter water can is among our top and most popular products, valued for its durability, capacity, and practicality. Trusted by customers nationwide, it remains in high demand throughout every season of the year, making it a reliable choice for both household and commercial use.

10-liter cooking oil can

Our 10-liter cooking oil can is one of our best-selling products, known for its robust design, secure sealing, and ease of handling. Trusted by food manufacturers, distributors, and households alike, it remains in high demand throughout the year, ensuring safe storage and transport of cooking oil in all seasons.

20L HDPE Plastic Can

Our 20-liter yellow HDPE plastic can is specially designed for storing and transporting cooking ghee in bulk quantities. Built from food-grade material, it ensures the purity and freshness of the ghee while maintaining excellent durability for long-distance transportation.

1L to 5L Motor Oil & Gear Oil Cans (Black / Grey / Blue)

High-quality, leak-proof, and chemical-resistant cans built for safe storage of motor and gear oils. Their sturdy design and ergonomic shape make them a preferred choice for lubricant companies across Pakistan, ensuring reliability and ease of use.
